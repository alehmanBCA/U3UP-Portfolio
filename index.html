*Click on the logo above to visit the projects Github*

What?

My Circuit Street was created in order to help employees and employers at a computer store. Our project took inspiration and therefore functions similarly to the Walmart app used by employees. My Circuit Street allows for employees at the store to be able to track the price and location of any and all items in the store with ease. It also allows for employers/managers to check the status (clocked in/clocked out) of employees at the store, which is very helpful for determining things such as pay.

How?

My Circuit Street was created using the programming language Python and the GUI Toolkit Tkinter. Tkinter is what was used to create the visual aspect of our website, it is responsible for everything that you see on the screen. Python is the language that was used in order to make everything actually work such as adding items and prices to the site.
